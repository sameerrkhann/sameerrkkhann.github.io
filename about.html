Our Story

A NEW CHAPTER IN LIGHTER MANUFACTURING

Spark Industries was founded in 2025 with a clear purpose — to fill a gap in India's lighter distribution market. While demand for quality lighters was growing across pan shops, kirana stores, and retail chains, reliable factory-direct supply remained elusive for most distributors.

We set up our manufacturing facility in Hosiery Complex Phase-2, Noida — one of the most strategically located industrial zones in the Delhi NCR region, with direct access to national highways and robust logistics infrastructure.

From day one, our focus has been simple: manufacture lighters that distributors are proud to sell and retailers love to stock. Two brands. One uncompromising standard of quality.

How We Make Them

THE MANUFACTURING PROCESS

Every lighter that leaves our Noida facility goes through a rigorous manufacturing and quality process.

01

Raw Material Sourcing

High-grade polymer body materials and precision metal components sourced from verified suppliers.

02

Body Moulding

Thick-walled body formation with reinforced structure for durability and drop resistance.

03

Gas Filling

Precision gas filling process — more gas per lighter for extended burn time and distributor value.

04

Ignition Assembly

High-quality flint ignition system assembly. Every unit tested for reliable first-strike performance.

05

Quality Control

Multi-point QC checks — flame test, body inspection, gas leak check, and packaging verification.

06

Packaging & Dispatch

Branded packaging applied (LightUp or KTM), box sealing, and dispatch to distributor warehouse.
