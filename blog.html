Why Lighter Quality Matters for Retailers

Retailers don't just sell products — they sell trust. When a customer buys a lighter that fails to ignite or runs out of gas in two days, they blame the shopkeeper, not the brand.

That's why supplying quality lighters matters for retailer loyalty. Thick walls, reliable ignition, and more gas per lighter are the three factors that determine whether a retailer reorders from you.
